About Me

Hello everybody! I'm originally from Ukraine. I am now living in Belgium for 6 years already. I graduated from the Theater College as ballet dancer and choreography teacher in Dnepropetrovsk. For 36 years I had the chance to work in different part of the world for international companies. In Russia, Israel, China, France, Germany, Turkey, Italy ... All my life i was always curious about technologies. 5 years ago, I started to think about changing my profession... and I discovered the world of Coding...it was unbelievably interesting for me straight away. I was addicted to Coding very quickly. Now, after 1.5 years of studying every day, I realized that a web developer has a lot in common with the artist I was before. Both jobs involve hard work that is hidden from the eyes of the audience, and the audience sees only the final result. Everything is exactly the same in the theater industry, the tech world is actually really close to my artistic world i knew so well before.
